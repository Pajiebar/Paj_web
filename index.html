Benefits of hiring a Virtual Assistant

There are many benefits to outsourcing the administrative duties for your company and some of these are summarized below:

Cost Effectiveness

– client incurs no payroll taxes, no insurance costs as well as no fringe benefit expenses. The client is not responsible for providing office space or equipment. This is contained in CS Administrative Firm hourly rate.

Professional Partner

– by working in partnership with you, we take the time to get to know your working style, needs and business. We also offer honest advice and feedback.

Confidentiality

– hiring freelance workers from different website will put your files and other information at risk. Private mobile phones are strictly prohibited in the work area. The office is monitored by CCTVs 24/7. We do not misuse information of our clients, our firms, or our people for personal advantage or for the benefit of third parties.

Reliability

– by working in partnership with you, we establish trust leaving you safe in the knowledge that a professional is handling your work for you. We are a team and we work hand in hand to provide the best service to our clients. In the event that the Virtual Assistant who is assigned to you will need to take some time off due to sickness or personal reasons, someone from our team will takeover the job.

Flexibility

– we offer solutions for all types of requirements whether you need work to be completed on a daily, weekly or monthly basis. In order to help with any ad hoc or specific projects you may have.

Back Up Support

– because we tailor our services to meet individual requirements, we are able to work alongside existing staff to undertake work when they are overstretched or on holiday.

Frees up your Valuable Time

– by outsourcing your tasks to your Virtual Executive Assistant, you can concentrate on important things like growing your business, achieving work / life balance, networking and more.
